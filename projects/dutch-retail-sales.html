Dutch Retail Sales (1960–1995): an Excel KPI Hub

2024
Business Intelligence

The Challenge

Historical datasets often suffer from fragmentation and legacy formatting. This project involved cleaning and aggregating decades of Dutch retail performance data to create a high-fidelity, interactive stakeholder dashboard.

Implementation

The solution features dynamic filtering, period-over-period growth tracking, and automated forecasting models built within a robust Excel architecture. This demonstrates senior-level capability in turning raw sales data into actionable business levers.
