Unmet Mental Health Need and Employment Outcomes

Exploratory Analysis • Data Visualization • Public Policy

Overview

This project explores the relationship between unmet mental health needs and employment outcomes, utilizing large-scale public health datasets to identify key intersections between mental wellness and economic stability.

Key Findings

The analysis reveals significant correlations between access to mental health services and career longevity, highlighting the economic imperative of integrated healthcare solutions.
