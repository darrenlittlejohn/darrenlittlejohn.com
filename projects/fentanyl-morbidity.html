Fentanyl Morbidity: Data Visualization in R

2024
R / Tidyverse

The Analysis

This project uses the R-Tidyverse ecosystem to map and analyze death rates and morbidity patterns related to Fentanyl usage. By cleaning complex medical datasets and applying geospatial mapping, I identified regional hotspots and demographic vulnerabilities.

Technical Stack

The project highlights proficiency in `ggplot2` for high-end visualization, `dplyr` for data manipulation, and geospatial packages for regional trend analysis.
